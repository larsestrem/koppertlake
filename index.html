Koppert Lake

Koppert Lake is Active Water Sports‘ premiere private lake in Toledo, Washington. It offers wakeboard and waterski competitions, private lessons, camping, a retail shop and more. Stay up-to-date on the latest events by signing up for AWS’ newsletter or contact Koppert Lake at 360-864-4137.
